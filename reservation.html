Reservation Policies

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged subject to availability.

Payment

A valid credit card is required to guarantee your reservation. Full payment is due upon check-in.

Cancellation

Cancellations must be made 48 hours prior to arrival to avoid a one-night room charge. For peak season bookings, cancellations must be made 72 hours in advance.

Extra Person

Additional guests beyond the standard occupancy will incur an extra charge of PKR 5,000 per person per night.

Our reservations team is available to help with any questions or special requirements.
